FAQs Page

Home > FAQs

How do I create an account?

To create an account, go to the Register page, fill in your details, and click the Register button. Once registered, you can log in and start shopping.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Kaspi, Halyk, BCC, and other secure online payment methods.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on your location. Typically, orders are delivered within 3-7 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order is shipped.

Can I return a product?

Yes! If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 14 days of delivery. The product must be unused and in its original packaging. Check our Return Policy for more details.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can only be modified or canceled before they are processed. Contact our Customer Support as soon as possible for assistance.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach out to our support team via email, live chat, or our contact form on the website.
